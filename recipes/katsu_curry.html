Chicken Katsu Curry

Recipe for a Katsu Curry!

This is a recipe for an easy, tasty and authentic chicken katsu curry. It will take around 20-30mins for the sauce, but it takes time to prep veg and rice. So set aside 1hr15mins approx overall.

Ingredients

  • S&B Golden Curry Japanese Curry Mix 92g (Medium or hot)
  • Half a large onion, or 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 or 3 carrots, diced
  • Sushi grade short-grain rice, 1/2 cup per person
  • 1 breaded chicken cutlet per person
  • Optional: one potato, diced

Steps

  1. Start to prepare rice:
    1. Put dry rice into pot and wash by running cold water and swirling it around with your fingers
    2. Pour out cloudy, starchy water until only a tiny amount is left. Repeat Steps 1 & 2 3-4 times until water becomes clear.
    3. Drain rice in a sieve, put it in a medium sized pot, and add 3/4 cup water per 1/2 cup of rice
    4. Leave rice to soak for 20-30 mins
    5. You could prep veg now to save time later
  2. After 30mins, cover rice with lid and bring to boil over a medium heat. This should take ~10 mins. You might want to start breaking up the curry mix block to save time later.
  3. At same time, add 1tbsp vegetable oil to a large pot and sweat onions over a medium heat for 5 mins
  4. Once the rice starts boiling set timer for 30 minutes and turn hob down to simmer.
  5. At the same time, add 750ml cold water to onions, add carrots & potato if using, and bring to boil over medium heat. This should take ~10 mins
  6. At 10 minute mark, preheat oven 200°C, 180°C Fan.
  7. After 13 mins has passed, take rice off heat and let it sit for at least 10 mins. Put chicken in oven,
  8. Meanwhile, cover large pot and simmer for 15 mins.
  9. Take large pot off heat & add the curry mix. Stir well and dissolve thoroughly. Put pot back on heat and simmer for 5 mins, stirring constantly.
  10. Dish out rice, ladle on curry, & by then the chicken should be cooked so take it out, cut it into slices, place on top of rice & and serve
